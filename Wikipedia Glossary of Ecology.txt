"<a href=""https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Negative_feedback_loop"" class=""mw-redirect"" title=""Negative feedback loop"">negative feedback loop</a>"|"

<dd class=""glossary"">Any delineation between <a href=""#habitat"">habitats</a> in which <a href=""https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tree"" title=""Tree"">trees</a> are capable of growing and in which they are not capable of growing.
